Our Goals

Environmental

Increased efficiency can lower greenhouse gas emissions and other pollutants, as well as decrease water used for Electricity Generation.

Economic

Improving energy efficiency reduces strain to already constrained generation capacity, and costs significantly less than investing in new generation and transmission assets. Reduced energy costs for consumers.

Utility System

When integrated into energy resource plans, energy efficiency can provide long-term benefits by lowering base load and peak demand and reducing the need for additional generation capacity.
